Guide

This website is used for the purpose of carrying out balance transactions in digital currency exchange.

1.Balance

This feature shows the various balances you have, such as: your last balance, transfer balance, income balance, balance from currency exchange, and real-time graphs of digital currency.

2.Transaction

This feature is used to carry out several transactions directly, here are some of the transactions used:

a. Top Up

A feature used to add to your balance to make currency exchange or payments.

b. Convert Currency

A feature used to exchange foreign currency, from your currency balance to another currency balance.

c. Payment

A feature used to pay your balance towards the balance that you will pay according to your currency balance.

d. Transaction History

A feature is used to show your history when making transactions on this website.

3.Account

This feature is used for your account settings to change some of your profile data. Here are some of the account settings used:

a. Profile Details

A feature used to manage and change some profile data from your account.

b. Security Account

The feature used in this security account is changing the new password for the account you are using.
